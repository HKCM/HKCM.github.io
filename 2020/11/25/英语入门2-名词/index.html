英语入门2-名词

Posted on 2020-11-25 Edited on 2020-11-30 In 英语 , 名词

描述: 名词

名词短语

a good boy 限定词 + 修饰词 + 名词

限定词构成

  1. 冠词 - a, an, the
  2. 形容词性的物主代词 - my, his, her, their
  3. 名词所有格 - Tom’s
  4. 指示代词 - these, that
  5. 不定代词 - some
  6. 基数词 - one, two
  7. 疑问代词 - what
限定词 修饰词 主体名词 注释
a good boy
a boy
good boys Countable 可数名词复数可以省略限定词
boys
good news [UnCountable] 不可数名词复数可以省略限定词
news
China 专有名词
Tom
The rich people

代词

人称代词

主格 宾格 形物代 名物代 反身代
I me my mine myself
you you your yours yourself
he him his his himself
she her her hers herself
it it its its itself
we us our ours ourselves
you you your yours yourselves
they them their theirs themselves

主格名词
I like english

宾格名词
It is me

形物代
my book,book是名词,修饰名词的是形容词,所以my就是形容词性的物主代词
This is my book

名物代
同上my book 是 形容词性物主代词 + 名词 = 名词性的物主代词 mine
This book is my book
This book is mine

反身代词
I love myself

指示代词

单数 复数
this these
that those

This is a book
These are books

That is a book
Those are books

that 可用于可数名词的单数和不可数名词

weather 为不可数名词
The weather of Chengdu is cooler than the weather of Chongqin.
The weather of Chengdu is cooler than that of Chongqin.

oranges 为复数
The oranges of Huainan are sweeter than the oranges of Huibei.
The oranges of Huainan are sweeter than those of Huibei.

疑问代词

数词

基数词

  1. 整数
    one twe three

  2. 小数
    1.3 one point 3

  3. 分数

    1. 分子为1
      三分之一: one third
    2. 分子大于1
      三分之二: two thirds

  4. 百分数
    100% one hundred percent

    Two thirds of the students in the class are girls. 三分之二是名词

    序数词(序数词)

    first second third

    He was the first one to top the mountain peak.
    The second student is a girl. 序数词做形容词

不定式
动名词
句子

名词分类

  1. 专有名词
    人名,地名,机构名 Tom, Shanghai
  2. 普通名词
    1. 可数名词
      1. 个体名词
        boy
      2. 集体名词
        family
    2. 不可数名词
      1. 物质名词
        milk, bread
      2. 抽象名词
        power

可数名词复数变化

名词 构成 示例
一般名词 + s map - maps, soldier - soldiers
以 s,x,sh,ch 结尾 + es bus - buses, box - boxes, brush - brushes, watch - watcher
辅音字母 + y 结尾 去y + ies baby - babies, lady - ladies
元音字母 + y 结尾 + s monkey - monkeys, toy - toys
f,fe 结尾 去f,fe + ves self - selves, knife - knives, roof - roofs
o 结尾有生命 + es hero - heroes, potato - potato potatoes
o 结尾无生命 + s radio - radios, piano - pianos

可数名词不规则复数变化
元音字母变化 man - men, foot - feet, goose - geese, tooth - teeth
单复同型 sheep, deer
特殊 mouse - mice, child - children, ox - oxen
更特殊 fish - fish: 表数目; fish - fishes: 表种类; fish - fish: 表鱼肉
chicken - chickens: 表数目; chicken - chicken: 表鸡肉
  1. 单数
  2. 复数
    • 规则变化
      • book - books
    • 不规则变化
      • German - Germans, American - Americans
      • Frenchman - Frenchmen, man - men
      • man teacher - men teachers

不可数名词

  1. 不变
  2. 量化
    • a glass of water
    • a pice of paper
  3. 可数化
    • have a good time
  4. 转义
    time - times 时间-次数 |exercise - exercises 练习-练习题,演习|paper - papers 纸 - 论文
    |
